Angry Animals is a physics-based puzzle game where you launch projectiles to destroy all the cups with as few attempts as possible.

Features:
- 100 challenging levels with increasing difficulty
- Physics-based slingshot mechanics
- Face customization (camera capture or gallery)
- Procedural cup layouts
- Free to play with optional premium unlock
- No ads in premium version
- No network required (except for ads)

Gameplay:
Launch projectiles using the slingshot to destroy all cups in each level. Earn stars based on your performance. Progress through 100 levels of increasing difficulty.
